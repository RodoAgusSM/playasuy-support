Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-04

Summary

PlayasUY does not collect, store, or share personal data.

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data Collection

PlayasUY does not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, location, identifiers, or usage analytics.

Feeds, preferences, and saved content are stored locally on the user’s device.

Third-Party Content

PlayasUY aggregates publicly available third-party content (e.g., RSS feeds, news sites, and Reddit) selected by the user.

Some third-party content may include sensitive topics (including adult themes or violence) depending on user-selected sources and filters. Sensitive content is not the core experience and can be restricted via in-app settings.

Purchases

If subscriptions or purchases are available, they are handled by Apple via In-App Purchases. PlayasUY does not receive payment details.
